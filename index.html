FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

What markers work with these books?

Any standard dot marker (dauber) brand works great — Do-a-Dot, Bingo daubers, or similar. The thick paper handles multiple fills without bleed-through.

What age range are these books for?

The Animals series is perfect from age 2 and up. The ABC book is designed for ages 1–5, making it ideal for building early letter recognition through art.

Can I order in bulk for a classroom or daycare?

Absolutely! Teachers and therapists love these books. For bulk orders of 10+ copies, reach out via the contact form below for pricing and shipping options.

Are these books good for kids with special needs?

Yes — the oversized dots and simple format are widely used by occupational therapists to build fine motor skills, focus, and color recognition in a low-pressure, joyful way.

How many pages are in each book?

The Animals books have 50+ activity pages each. The ABC book has 60+ pages — one spread per letter, plus bonus pages. All are printed on quality paper stock.

Are more books coming?

Yes! New themes are in the works. Sign up for the free sample above to be first to know when new books launch.
