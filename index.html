About the Author

Making creativity

I create dot marker activity books that give kids a screen-free creative outlet. Every page is designed with big, easy-to-fill dots so little hands feel confident and proud of what they make.

My books are loved by parents, teachers, and therapists — anywhere kids need calm, focused, joyful play. Because every child deserves to feel like an artist.
