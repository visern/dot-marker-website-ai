# About the Author — Veronica Daves

Veronica Daves is a passionate early childhood educator and creator of
playful learning tools for toddlers and preschoolers. With a focus on
hands-on, screen-free activities, she designs books that spark creativity
while building real developmental skills.

Her Dot Marker Learning Library series brings joy, focus, and growth to
kids ages 1–5 — one colorful page at a time.
