# About the Author — Veronica Daves

Dot Marker Books was created by Veronica Daves, a passionate early
childhood educator who designs playful, screen-free learning tools for
toddlers and preschoolers. With a focus on hands-on activities, she
designs books that spark creativity while building real developmental
skills.

Her Dot Marker Learning Library series brings joy, focus, and growth to
kids ages 1–5 — one colorful page at a time.
