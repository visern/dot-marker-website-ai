# Frequently Asked Questions

## What markers work with these books?
Any standard dot marker (dauber) brand works great, such as Do-a-Dot or
Bingo daubers. The thick paper handles multiple fills without bleed-through.

## What age range are these books for?
The Animals series is perfect from age 2 and up. The ABC book is designed
for ages 1–5, making it ideal for building early letter recognition through
art.

## Can I order in bulk for a classroom or daycare?
Yes. Teachers and therapists love these books. For bulk orders of 10+
copies, customers should reach out via the contact form for pricing and
shipping options.

## Are these books good for kids with special needs?
Yes. The oversized dots and simple format are widely used by occupational
therapists to build fine motor skills, focus, and color recognition in a
low-pressure, joyful way.

## How many pages are in each book?
The Animals books have 50+ activity pages each. The ABC book has 60+ pages,
one spread per letter plus bonus pages. All books are printed on quality
paper stock.

Yes, new themes are in the works. Signing up for the free sample pack is
the best way to be first to know when new books launch.
