# Dot Markers Activity Book — Animals

Part of the Jungle Animals Series, this is the sequel to the original
"Dot Markers Activity Book — Ocean Animals" book — a 108-page collection of 54 animal-themed
dot marker pages for toddlers and preschoolers ages 1–5.

Spark creativity, learning, and joy — one dot at a time! Step into a
colorful safari with this activity book: over 50+ animal-themed dot marker
pages combining playful fun with skill-building activities. From farmyard
favorites to jungle giants, each page features big, bold dots perfectly
sized for tiny hands and dauber-style markers — works with Dauber Dawgs,
Dab O Ink, or any dot marker brand.

Why parents and teachers love this book:
- Screen-free creative fun — keeps kids engaged with mess-free, imaginative art
- Builds fine motor skills — supports hand-eye coordination, grip strength, and control
- Early learning ready — reinforces color recognition, visual focus, and pattern discovery
- Adorable animal themes — makes every page a story, promoting early language and play
- Perfect gift for toddlers — ideal for birthdays, holidays, preschool prep, or classroom use
