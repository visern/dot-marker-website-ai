# Dot Markers — ABC

Watch your little one light up with every dot! This Dot Markers Activity
Book turns learning the ABCs into joyful, mess-free fun that toddlers and
preschoolers can do all by themselves. Each page features one big, friendly
picture filled with large, easy-to-reach circles — perfectly sized for
little hands and standard dot markers (dot daubers / bingo markers). As
your child dabs each dot, they build fine motor skills, color recognition,
hand-eye coordination, and early focus, all while falling in love with
letters and first words.

## What's inside

- The full A to Z alphabet — one adorable picture per letter (A is for Apple, B is for Bear, C is for Cow...)
- Uppercase AND lowercase letters to dot and fill
- A first word on every page to build early vocabulary
- Bonus picture pages — cute animals, nature, food, and toys for endless dotting fun
- 114 pages of activities to keep little ones happily busy

## Why parents & teachers love it

- Big, simple dots — designed for ages 2, 3, 4, and 5, with no tiny shapes to frustrate beginners
- Single-sided pages so colors never bleed through to the next activity
- Mess-free learning — no paint cups, no water, no clean-up
- Builds real skills — letters, first words, colors, and pencil-grip readiness
- Screen-free quiet time for home, preschool, daycare, travel, and restaurants
