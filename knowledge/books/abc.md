# Dot Markers — ABC

Part of the ABC Learning Series, this is the newest release in the Dot Marker
Learning Library. It teaches the alphabet from A to Z through dot painting —
every letter gets its own bold, colorful page, paired with a simple object
that starts with that letter (Apple for A, Bear for B, Cow for C, and so on
through the full alphabet).

It's designed for ages 1–5, making it a good fit for building early letter
recognition through hands-on art rather than screens. Like the rest of the
collection, it uses oversized dot circles that are easy for little hands to
fill, so toddlers can complete a page with confidence on the first try.

It's sold in paperback on Amazon.
