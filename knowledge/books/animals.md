# Dot Markers — Animals

Part of the Ocean Animals Series, this book introduces children to sea life
through dot painting — sharks, fish, octopus, dolphins, and other ocean
animals, each with its own big, bold dot pattern.

The illustrations are designed for beginning dot marker users: patterns are
large and simple enough that toddlers can fill them in confidently on the
very first try, without needing steady fine motor control yet.

Recommended for ages 2 and up. Sold in paperback on Amazon.
